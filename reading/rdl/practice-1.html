Subject: Textbook Pickup — Important Deadlines
From: Westlake University Bookstore <bookstore@westlake.edu> To: All Students — Fall 2025 Date: August 18, 2025

Dear Students,

Your pre-ordered textbooks are now available for pickup at the campus bookstore, located on the first floor of the Student Union Building. Please bring your student ID and order confirmation email when you come to collect your books. The pickup window runs from August 20 through September 5.

If you need to exchange a textbook for a different edition, you may do so within 10 days of pickup, provided the book is in its original condition. Refunds are not available for pre-orders, but store credit will be issued for any cancellations made before August 25.

Best regards,
Westlake University Bookstore

IMPORTANT: Scheduled Building Maintenance

Riverside Hall — Student Housing Office

Dear Riverside Hall Residents,

The Student Housing Office would like to inform you that annual maintenance work will be carried out in Riverside Hall from September 15 to September 22. This maintenance includes inspection and repair of the heating and cooling systems, replacement of fire alarm batteries, and repainting of all common areas on floors 1 through 3.

During this period, the following temporary changes will be in effect:

  • The first-floor study lounge will be closed from September 15 to 18 for repainting.
  • Hot water may be unavailable for up to two hours each morning (6:00 AM to 8:00 AM) while heating systems are inspected.
  • The basement laundry room will remain open, but access through the south entrance will be restricted. Please use the north entrance instead.

Residents do not need to leave their rooms during maintenance, but we ask that you keep hallways clear of personal belongings to allow workers to move freely. If you have any concerns or special needs, please contact the Housing Office at housing@riverside.edu or visit Room 102 during office hours.

Thank you for your patience and cooperation.

Student Housing Office
Riverside Hall
