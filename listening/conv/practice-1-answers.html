Conversation 1 — IT Help Desk

場面：IT Help Desk ｜ 学生（女性）+ ITスタッフ（男性）

📜 スクリプトを見る（クリックで展開）
[Student] Hi, I'm having trouble connecting to the campus Wi-Fi on my laptop. It was working fine yesterday.
[Staff] I see. Have you tried restarting your laptop? Sometimes that clears up connection issues.
[Student] Yeah, I did that already, but it still won't connect. It keeps saying "authentication failed."
[Staff] That usually means your network password has expired. We reset all student passwords at the beginning of each month for security purposes.
[Student] Oh, I didn't know that. How do I set a new one?
[Staff] You can reset it through the student portal, or I can do it for you right here. You'll just need your student ID.
[Student] That would be great. Here's my ID. I have a class in twenty minutes, so I really need this fixed quickly.
[Staff] No problem. It'll only take a minute. I'll also send you an email reminder before next month's reset so you won't have this issue again.
[Student] That's really helpful, thank you so much!
1
Why does the student visit the IT help desk?
Detail
(A) To report a stolen laptop on campus.
(B) To install new software on her computer.
(C) To request a new student ID card.
(D) To get help connecting to the campus Wi-Fi.
解説： 学生の最初の発言で「having trouble connecting to the campus Wi-Fi」と明確に述べている。会話の冒頭で目的が示される典型的なパターン。
🔑 目的問題（Why does the student...）は冒頭に答えがある。最初の発話に全集中！
2
What will the staff member do for the student?
Detail
(A) Reset her network password and send a monthly reminder.
(B) Replace her laptop battery right away.
(C) Give her a temporary Wi-Fi hotspot device.
(D) Schedule a follow-up appointment for tomorrow.
解説： スタッフは「I can do it for you right here」（パスワードリセット）と「I'll also send you an email reminder」（月次リマインダー）の2つを提案。両方含む(A)が正解。
🔑 解決策問題は会話の後半に答えがある。2つのアクションが含まれる選択肢に注目。

Conversation 2 — Campus Bookstore

場面：Campus Bookstore ｜ 学生（男性）+ 書店スタッフ（女性）

📜 スクリプトを見る（クリックで展開）
[Student] Excuse me, I ordered a textbook online for pickup, but I received the wrong edition.
[Staff] Oh, I'm sorry about that. Do you have your order confirmation?
[Student] Yes, here it is. I ordered the third edition of Introduction to Psychology, but I got the second edition instead.
[Staff] Let me check our inventory. Unfortunately, we're currently out of stock on the third edition.
[Student] That's a problem. My professor specifically said we need the third edition because some chapters were reorganized.
[Staff] I understand. We have a new shipment arriving on Wednesday. I can reserve a copy for you and give you a ten percent discount for the inconvenience.
[Student] Wednesday works. Can I keep the second edition until then, just so I can start reading the parts that didn't change?
[Staff] Of course. Just bring it back when you pick up the new one, and we'll process the exchange then.
3
What can be inferred about why the student needs the third edition?
Inference
(A) The second edition contains too many errors.
(B) His professor requires it because the chapters were reorganized.
(C) The third edition is cheaper than the second.
(D) The bookstore no longer carries the second edition.
解説： 学生は「My professor specifically said we need the third edition because some chapters were reorganized」と述べている。直接的には教授の指示だが、推測すると章の再編成が理由。
🔑 推論問題：話者の発言から「なぜ」を推測する。「because」の後に理由がある。
4
What will the student most likely do next?
Inference
(A) Return the book and wait for a full refund.
(B) Buy the second edition for the whole semester.
(C) Read the second edition until Wednesday and then exchange it.
(D) Order the book from a different online bookstore.
解説： 学生は「Can I keep the second edition until then, just so I can start reading」と述べ、スタッフは「Just bring it back when you pick up the new one」と了承。水曜日まで第2版を読み、交換する流れが推測できる。
🔑 推論問題：「What will...most likely do next?」は会話の結論から次のアクションを推測する。
