📋 問題の確認
Scenario: You are a university student. Your grandmother has become seriously ill, and you need to travel home to be with your family for at least a week. You have a research paper due in Professor Clarke's sociology class in three days, and you will not be able to complete it on time.

Write an email to Professor Clarke. In your email:

  • Point 1 Explain your situation and why you cannot submit the paper on time.
  • Point 2 Request a specific extension and propose a new deadline.
  • Point 3 Describe what you have already completed on the paper to show your commitment.
✅ 模範回答（約95語）
Dear Professor Clarke,

1 I am writing to inform you that my grandmother has become seriously ill, and I need to travel home immediately to be with my family. Unfortunately, I will not be able to submit the research paper by the due date this Friday.

2 I would like to request an extension of one week. Would it be possible to submit the paper by next Friday instead?

3 I have already completed the outline and gathered all of my sources. I am confident that I can finish the paper as soon as I return.

Thank you for your understanding.
Best regards
✅ 自己チェックリスト
☑️ 3つのポイントがすべて含まれている
☑️ 各ポイントに2〜3文の説明がある
☑️ Dear [Name] で始まり、Best regards で終わっている
☑️ 80〜120語の範囲に収まっている
☑️ スペルミス・文法ミスがない
☑️ カジュアルすぎる表現を避けている（Hey! → Dear）
⚠️ よくあるミス
✗ ポイント見落とし

3つのうち1つを書き忘れる → 大減点。書き終わったら必ず再確認

✗ 冗長な前置き

"I hope this email finds you well..." は不要。すぐに目的を述べる

✗ カジュアルすぎる表現

"Hey Prof!" → "Dear Professor" に。メールの適切なトーンを意識

✗ 書きすぎ

120語を超えても加点なし。簡潔に3ポイントを網羅することが重要
